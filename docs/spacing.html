Spacing

How it works

Assign margin and padding properties to your components with a simple String respecting a predefined scale customizable

Margin

  • m Margin all
  • mt Margin top
  • mb Margin bottom
  • mr Margin right
  • ml Margin left
  • my Margin top & margin bottom
  • mx Margin lett & margin right

Pading

  • p Padding all
  • pt Padding top
  • pb Padding bottom
  • pr Padding right
  • pl Padding left
  • py Padding top & padding bottom
  • px Padding lett & padding right

Apply scale

To apply a scale you must add it after specifying a property {prop}-{scale}.

  • The property can be any:margin or padding predefined
  • The scale value can be: auto || Integer >= 1
